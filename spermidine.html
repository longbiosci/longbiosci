Spermidine

Autophagy inducer and longevity compound

What is Spermidine?

Spermidine is a polyamine compound found in foods like wheat germ, soybeans, and aged cheese. It has been shown to induce autophagy - the cellular "cleanup" process that removes damaged components.

Research suggests spermidine may have anti-aging benefits by promoting cellular renewal and longevity.
