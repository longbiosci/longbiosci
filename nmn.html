NMN / NAD+

β-Nicotinamide mononucleotide, NAD+ precursor

What is NMN?

β-Nicotinamide mononucleotide (NMN) is a nucleotide derived from niacin (vitamin B3). It serves as a precursor to NAD+ (Nicotinamide Adenine Dinucleotide), a coenzyme found in all living cells that declines with age.

NMN supplementation has been shown to increase NAD+ levels and is being studied for potential anti-aging benefits.
